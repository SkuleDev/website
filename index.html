About Us

We are here, Nothing to fear


Skule dev is an programming club for engineers who are interested in software and innovation. The software ecosystem is one with many platforms which cannot be mastered by a single person. We aim at bringing together programmers in different fields and use their talents to develop something massive and awesome.

The members shall either be a developer or project manager (similar to development teams in many IT companies). As a developer, you shall be coding, designing and shaping the project. The member can also choose to be a project manager who will help with the development of the project but will also be responsible for communicating with the different teams involved and tracking the progress of their team.

The members shall gain valuable experience by developing projects with the club which can be added to their portfolios, making them more industry ready and desirable to recruiters.
